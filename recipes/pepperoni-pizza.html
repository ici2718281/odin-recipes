Pepperoni Pizza

Description

This pepperoni pizza recipe produces a quick and easy classic! Delicious homemade pizza has never been easier.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Pizza sauce
  • Pepperoni slices
  • Mozzarella

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220 degrees Celsius. Grease two 12-inch pizza pans.
  2. Make crust: Combine 2 cups flour, yeast, sugar, and salt in a large bowl. Add warm water and oil; mix until well blended, about 1 minute. Gradually add remaining flour, a little at a time, until a soft, sticky dough forms.
  3. Transfer dough to a floured surface; knead until dough is smooth and elastic, about 4 minutes. Add more flour as needed.
  4. Divide dough in half. Lightly flour your hands, then pat each piece of dough onto the prepared pizza pans.
  5. Top dough with sauce, cheese, and pepperoni.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until crusts are browned and cheese is bubbly, 18 to 20 minutes. Rotate pizza pans between the top and bottom oven racks halfway through baking.
